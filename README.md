**Author / Verification:** 23f1001281@ds.study.iitm.ac.in

## Dataset
Quarterly customer retention rates for 2024:
- Q1: 67.11
- Q2: 68.79
- Q3: 69.12
- Q4: 74.59

**Average (2024): 69.9**

**Industry target:** 85

## Key findings
- Slow, steady increases Q1→Q3; larger uplift in Q4 (74.59).
- Company average (69.9) is ~15.1 percentage points below the industry target of 85.
- Trend is positive but insufficient to reach 85 without targeted action.

## Business implications
- Revenue at risk due to lower retention vs benchmark.
- Prioritize high-impact retention levers.

## Recommended solution
**implement targeted retention campaigns**

### Tactical recommendations
1. Churn-risk segmentation & targeted offers.
2. Personalized lifecycle campaigns.
3. Loyalty tiers & rewards.
4. Win-back flows + exit surveys.
5. A/B tests for each campaign.
6. Operationalize KPIs (dashboarding).
